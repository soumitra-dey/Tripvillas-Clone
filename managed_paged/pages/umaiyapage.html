Explore The Community

Think Goa & you’re transported to a land of eternal sunshine, surf and a never ending celebration called life. Spread across 4.82 acres of verdant landscape, Rio de Goa pays a tribute to all of this & also provides you a resort-like lifestyle with luxurious amenities, a lavish multi-level clubhouse and a central active green zone. Designed by an internationally acclaimed architectural firm HB Design, Rio de Goa is a Spanish themed gated community. The development consists of 14 towers of 8 floors each, an intimate high-rise architecture with distinctively conceptualized homes. Every aspect of Rio-De-Goa is designed & conceptualized for better living with no dearth of fun.
